38. tolong bat nih contoh soal usbn Tengs


tan 30° = FB/BD
√3 / 3 = 4/BD
BD√3 = 12
BD = 12/√3 × √3 / √3
BD = 4√3 cm

BC = AB = CD = ½ BD
= ½ × 4√3
= 2√3 cm

tan 30° = EC/CD
√3 / 3 = EC/2√3
3EC = 6
EC = 2 cm

GF = FE = ED

sin 30° = EC/ED
1/2 = 2/ED
ED = 4 cm

(Boleh pake metode segitiga pythagoras 1, √3 , 2 jika tidak mau pake cara trigonometri)

Keliling ABFG = EC + AB + BF + ED
= 2 + 2√3 + 4 + 4
= 10 + 2√3
= 2(5 + √3) cm
