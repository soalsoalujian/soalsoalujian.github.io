Jawaban pertanyaan89 R = 111,25 C40 C = 32 RPembahasan jawaban

Perubahan °Reamur ke °Celcius

[tex] \color{aqua}89 R = 5/4 × 89 \\

\color{yellow}= (5 × 89)/4 \\ \color{pink}

= 445/4 \\

\color{red} = 111,25 C [/tex]

Perubahan °Celcius ke °Reamur

[tex] \color{blue}40 C = 4/5 × 40 \\

\color{orange}= (4 × 40)/5 \\ \color{green}

= 160/ 5 \\

\color{purple}= 32 R[/tex]
