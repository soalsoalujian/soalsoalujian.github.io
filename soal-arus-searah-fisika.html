Penjelasan:

4Ω, 6Ω dan 12Ω di paralel

1/Rp = 1/4 + 1/6 + 1/12

1/Rp = 6/12

Rp = 12/6 = 2 Ω

Hambatan total

Rt = Rp + 5 + 1 = 2 + 5 + 1

Rt = 8 Ω

Arus total

It = E/Rt = 12/8 = 1,5 A

Tegangan di hambatan paralel

Vp = It . Rp = 1,5 . 2 = 3 V

Arus di setiap cabang

I₂ = Vp/4 = 3/4 = 0,75 A

I₃ = Vp/6 = 3/6 = 0,5 A

I₄ = Vp/12 = 3/12 = 0,25 A

I₁ = It = 1,5 A
