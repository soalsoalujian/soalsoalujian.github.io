6. Apa bedanya ask bertanya dan ask Meminta?


ANSWER
__________
KATEGORI : PENGGUNAAN KATA "ASK" + PERBEDAANNYA
TINGKAT : SMP/SMA


=> ASK
jika diikuti oleh FOR maka artinya MEMINTA
contoh:
- she comes to my house because she wants to ask for a help.

=> ASK
jika diikuti oleh TO maka artinya MEMINTA
- She asks me to do her homework.

10. 5 Contoh soal Expression to ask habit and routines, dan Expression to ask frequency​


Jawaban:

Expression to ask habit and routines

What are your daily routines like?

Can you tell me about your typical morning routine?

What habits do you have before going to bed?

What do you usually do in your free time?

Can you describe a typical week in your life, including your work and leisure activities?

How often do you exercise?

How frequently do you eat out?

How many times a month do you visit your parents?

Do you travel often? If so, how often?
