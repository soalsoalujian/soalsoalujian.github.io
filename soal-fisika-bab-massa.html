v = (2i+7j) m/s
v = (-i+3j)/s
∆t = 2 s
a = __?

Percepatan rata-rata
a = ∆v / ∆t
a = [-i + 3j - (2i+7j)] / 2
a = (-3i - 4j) / 2

|a| = √((-1,5)² + (-2)²)

Penyelesaian

P . t = 1/2 . m . (Vt^2 - Vo^2)
6000 . t = 1/2 . 900 . (20^2 - 10^2)
6000 t = 450 . (400 - 100)
6000 t = 450 . 300
6000 t = 135000
t = 135000/6000
t = 22,5 sDari rumus usaha
W = FΔx
FΔx = maΔx ... (*)

v₂² = v₁² + 2aΔx
v₂² - v₁² = 2aΔx
(v₂² - v₁²) / 2 = aΔx ... (**)
Substitusi persamaan (**) ke (*)
FΔx = m[(v₂² - v₁²) / 2]
W = 1/2 m(v₂² - v₁²)
Karena P = W / t, maka W = Pt
Pt = 1/2 m(v₂² - v₁²)

t = [m(v₂² - v₁²)] / (2P)
  = [900(20² - 10²)] / [2(8.750)]
  = 22,5 s
