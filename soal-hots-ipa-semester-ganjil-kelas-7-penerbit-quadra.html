› NOMOR 11

Salah satu akar persamaan kuadrat x² – 15x + p = 0 adalah x = 3. Nilai p yang memenuhi adalah...

Penyelesaian:

x² – 15x + p = 0

3² – 15(3) + p = 0

9 – 45 + p = 0

–36 + p = 0

ㅤㅤㅤp = 36 ✅

Opsi Jawaban : D

› NOMOR 12

Nilai diskriminan persamaan kuadrat 2x² – 3x + 15 = 0 adalah...

Penyelesaian:

2x² – 3x + 15 = 0

a = 2b = –3c = 15

Sehingga nilai diskriminannya adalah:

D = b² – 4ac

ㅤ= (–3)² – 4(2)(15)

ㅤ= 9 – 4(2)(15)

ㅤ= 9 – 120

ㅤ= –111 ✅

Opsi Jawaban : B

› NOMOR 13

Persamaan kuadrat yang mempunyai dua akar real berbeda adalah....

Penyelesaian:

Persamaan kuadrat yang mempunyai dua akar real berbeda jika D > 0.

(i) x² – 5x + 9 = 0

ㅤD = b² – 4ac

ㅤㅤ= (–5)² – 4(1)(9)

ㅤㅤ= 25 – 36

ㅤㅤ= –11ㅤ→ㅤD < 0

(ii) x² + 9x – 10 = 0

ㅤD = b² – 4ac

ㅤㅤ= 9² – 4(1)(–10)

ㅤㅤ= 81 + 40

ㅤㅤ= 121ㅤ→ㅤD > 0

(iii) 2x² – 2x – 15 = 0

ㅤ D = b² – 4ac

ㅤㅤ = (–2)² – 4(2)(–15)

ㅤㅤ = 4 + 120

ㅤㅤ = 124ㅤ→ㅤD > 0

Jadi, persamaan kuadrat yang mempunyai dua akar real berbeda adalah (ii) dan (iii).

Opsi Jawaban : C

Climate change is a pressing global issue that requires immediate action. It is causing a rise in global temperatures, resulting in melting ice caps, sea-level rise, and extreme weather events. The consequences of climate change are far-reaching and impact both human and natural systems. To mitigate the effects of climate change, it is crucial to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, promote renewable energy sources, and implement sustainable practices.

Questions:
