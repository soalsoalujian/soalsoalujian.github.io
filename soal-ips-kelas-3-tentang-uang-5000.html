7. Jika uang a 1 2/3 b dan uang b 1 1/2 c,jika uang a 5000.berapakah uang mereka?



[tex]a = 1 \frac{2}{3} b \\ 5000 = \frac{5}{3} b \\ b = 5000 \times \frac{3}{5} \\ b = 3000[/tex]
[tex]b = 1 \frac{1}{2} c \\ 3000 = \frac{3}{2} c \\ c = 3000 \times \frac{2}{3} \\ c = 2000[/tex]
