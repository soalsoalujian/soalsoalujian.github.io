a = 35 - 36 + 3

38. matematika klas 7 smp

Bab = Bangun Datar
Materi = Segitiga
------------------------------------------------- ✌

Luas Segitiga DEF
= ½ x a x t
= ½ x 14 cm x 12 cm
= 7 cm x 12 cm
= 84 cm²

EF = √(12 cm)² + (5 cm)²
EF = √144 cm² + 25 cm²
EF = √169 cm²
EF = 13 cm

Keliling Segitiga DEF
= DE + EF + DF
= 14 cm + 13 cm + 21 cm
= 27 cm + 21 cm
