16. Soal UAS Sleman Kelas 9


(9^x . b² . 7^y)^4 = 9^20 . b^z . 7^8

9^(4x) . b^8 . 7^(4y) = 9^20 . b^z . 7^8
4x = 20
x = 5

z = 8

4y = 8
y = 2

maka, x + y + z = 5 + 2 + 8 = 15

jawabannya a
