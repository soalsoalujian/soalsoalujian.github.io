1. D. Kuat Arus

2. D. 11 Ohm

Penjelasan singkat: Rtot = R1 + R2 + R3 = 2 + 3+ 6 = 11 Ohm

3. D. 3,5 A

Penjelasan singkat: Rtot = R1 + R2 + R3 = 3 + 4+ 5 = 12 Ohm

                                Arus = V / Rtot = 42 / 12 = 3,5 A

4. E. 4 Ohm (Gambar kurang jelas, tidak ada keterangan R1-4)

Penjelasan singkat: misal R1 R2 resistor atas, R3 R4 resistor bawah

R12 = R1 + R2 = 2+4 = 6 ohm

R34 = R3 +R4 = 4 + 8 = 12 ohm

Rtot = (R12 + R34)^-1 = (1/6 +1/12)^-1 = (3/12)^-1 = (1/4)^-1 =4 ohm

39. Pertanyaan FisikaMateri : Listrik DinamisKelas : XII SMASoal : Pada gambar




GGL total
ΣE = E1 + E2 + E3
ΣE = -8 + 6 + 8 = 6 V

Hambatan dalam total
Σr = 0,5 + 0,5 + 1 = 2 ohm

Hambatan luar total
ΣR = 2 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 4 + 8
ΣR = 23 ohm

Kuat arus
i = ΣE / (ΣR + Σr)
i = 6 / (23 + 2)
i = 0,24 A

W = 39,744 J

Wr = 0,24 • 2 • 30 = 3,456 J

However, the boy was still breathing. Umar could see that the boy had bruises all over his face and arms. Umar did not know what to do. Few cars passed along this road at this time of the day.
